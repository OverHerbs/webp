    Triphla

    "Trifla," also known as "Triphala," is an ancient herbal formulation from traditional Ayurvedic medicine. It is composed of three fruits: Amla (Emblica officinalis), Haritaki (Terminalia chebula), and Bibhitaki (Terminalia bellirica). Each of these fruits has its own unique health benefits, and when combined in trifla, they are believed to synergistically enhance their therapeutic effects.
